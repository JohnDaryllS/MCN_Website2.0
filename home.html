CREDIT OVERSIGHT AND LOAN OPERATIONS: Classifying Risks and Building Provisions
This training emphasizes the critical processes within credit management, focusing on the accurate assessment of credit risk and the establishment of neccessary financial provisions. Proper classification of risk allows financial provisions. Proper classification of risk allows financial institutions to identify potential problem loans early; while building provisions ensures they are financially prepared to cover any future losses. Together, these training helps maintain the stability of the institution, mitigate financial risk, and ensure compliance with regulatory standards, ultimately contributing to stronger credit operations and better decision-making.
Schedule: April 24, 2025 - 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM
Location:
Brokenshire Hotel Resort and Convention Center
Target Participants:
Credit Manager/ Officer, Loan Officer, Internal Auditor, Compliance Officer, Treasurer, Accountants, CFO - Chief Finance Officer, BOD's
