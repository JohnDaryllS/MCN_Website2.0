Requirements for Membership Application

  • Accomplished Membership Application
  • Board Resolution (contains intent to apply for membership, as well as the Official Representative and Alternate Representative)
  • CDA Certificate of Registration
  • Articles of Cooperation (and Amendments, if any)
  • By-Laws (and Amendments, if any)
  • Audited FS (last 3 years) and Latest Interim Financial Statement
  • List of Board of Directors, Officers, and General Manager/CEO
  • COC
